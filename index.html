Claire Ambler Weddings

I am an enthusiastic and energetic individual who loves weddings and organisation, and specialise in weddings and events offering a personal service and great attention to detail with a smile.

You will also be dealing with up to an average of around 15 suppliers to coordinate.  I have the pleasure of working with some great suppliers across the UK specifically in the Home Counties where I am based. I work with these local, reliable and cost effective suppliers to source the right supplier that suits you .

I am all about cost saving and being efficient and I can help you to avoid the huge markups and unnecessary spend that usually appear when you mention the work 'wedding'!  I can work with you as much or as little as you like, from total wedding management, 8 weeks leading up towards the wedding or just 'on the day management, the choice is yours.  

I offer a free of charge no obligation Consultation to met with you to decide if you think we can work together, after all, hiring a wedding coordinator is all about the relationship and trust.

I always pride myself in offering you 100% commitment and support from the outset to build your dream wedding day.
