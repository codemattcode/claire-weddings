Welcome

I provide a professional, friendly and discrete service to clients and pride myself in my attention to detail and my own high standards help to ensure that no stone lays unturned to ensure your wedding or occasion day runs as smoothly as possible. I am also there to relieve any stresses for you in the planning process so you can enjoy your day

I would love to hear from you if you feel that I can be of assistance. The first step is to contact me so we can arrange a free of charge no obligation consultation at your home or wherever you would like to meet. In wedding planning, synergy between the wedding coordinator and the bride and her fiance is key to a successful relationship after all, its months of planning together.

I will be with you every step of the way from our initial consultaion to ascertain what level of involvement you wish me to have to liasing with other suppliers on your behalf ensuring that you are involved as much as or as litte as you wish. After all this is your wedding day and I am with you to act in an advisory role and not to take over.

I have a whole host of like-minded experts in their own field with whom I work alongside providing you with all the elements you need to bring your wedding day together.

About Me

I am an experienced wedding and events consultant with over 25 years experience. I've realised over the years that I have the abilility to put people at ease and help them to achieve their ultimate dreams. My goal is to ensure that couples have an amazing, memorable and enjoyable day. Whether I am planning the entire event or just applying the finishing touches, I am professional and committed.

My knowledge and expertise means that you can rely on my advice and I work closely with my couples to deliver their perfect day but never taking over. My business stands for quality, perfection and an attention to detail second to none - all at an affordable cost! Your wedding is one of the biggest days of your life and you want it to be perfect and memorable.

Perhaps at first glance hiring me to plan your wedding with you may seem a little extravagant but in the long term it will save you time, money and of course stress!

I am an enthusiastic and energetic individual who loves weddings and organisation, and specialise in weddings and events offering a personal service and great attention to detail with a smile.

My fees are based on a daily rate and you can always assume about 10-15% of your total wedding budget will be spent in the planning. An average wedding in 2016 is in the region of 21k with around 250 to 300 hours depending on the complexity of the wedding and your requirements.

You will also be dealing with up to an average of around 15 suppliers to coordinate.  I have the pleasure of working with some great suppliers across the UK specifically in the Home Counties where I am based. I work with these local, reliable and cost effective suppliers to source the right supplier that suits you .

I am all about cost saving and being efficient and I can help you to avoid the huge markups and unnecessary spend that usually appear when you mention the work 'wedding'!  I can work with you as much or as little as you like, from total wedding management, 8 weeks leading up towards the wedding or just 'on the day management, the choice is yours.  

I offer a free of charge no obligation Consultation to met with you to decide if you think we can work together, after all, hiring a wedding coordinator is all about the relationship and trust.

I always pride myself in offering you 100% commitment and support from the outset to build your dream wedding day. I am here to relieve you of the stress and anxiety of all the planning, allowing you to concentrate on the fun elements of your  wedding day. I will work with you to understand what you would like your day to be like getting to know you and your partner is a key part of my role to enable me to deliver the day you have dreamed of.
